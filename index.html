Biography

Welcome to the academic page of Wensheng Cheng.
My profile: resume, undergraduate transcript, master transcript.

I am currently a final year (Sep 2019 - June 2020) Master of Science in Engineering student in the School of Electronic Information of Wuhan University (GPA: 4.0/4.0), a top 10 university of China (Wiki). I am expected to graduate in June, 2020. I major in Information and Communication Engineering. I am supervised by Prof. Wen Yang. I also received the B. Eng degree from the School of Electronic Information in Wuhan University in 2017 (grades:87.89/100, rank: 4th/120).

My research lies at image analysis, especially aerial imagery. I also have experience in robertics vision, including UAV and robocar application. Recently, I am working on semantic segmentation methods for remote sensing imagery analysis. I also have interest in sharing projects and I am one of the main contributors of open source semantic sgmentation benchmark-semantic segmentation suite. This is one of the most popular semantic segmentation project on GitHub with over 1,900 stars.

I plan to continue my academic career and look for a Ph.D position starting in Fall, 2020. If you are interestd, welcome to contact me.
